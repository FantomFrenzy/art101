Lab 12 - Conditionals

Challenge

The challenge for this lab was to use conditionals, a string inputted by the user, and the modulus operator to produce different outputs based on the length of the input.

Problems

We encountered very little problems while implementing conditionals and understood the basics of how they work very easily.

Reflection

This lab allowed us to experiment with conditionals and learn about the modulus/remainder operator and how we can use these to display different outputs.
