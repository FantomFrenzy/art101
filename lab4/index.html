Lab 4 - Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

The lab had us thinking of how to do every task and how to make a rock-paper-scissors game on the computer. Then we created psuedocode for these tasks The lecture for pseudocoding was very helpful in showing us the process.

Problems

We did not have many problems with the lab. The only minor issue we had were making sure that the formatting of our lists was right and coming up with all the steps we would need to take to make pizza and create the rock-paper-scissor game.

Reflection

This lab helped us familiarize ourselves with the process of pseudocoding, which will let us better plan out our code in the future. We had to think very hard about what steps we would need to put on the lists. We also learned how to center an image on the page with CSS by looking at W3Schools.com.

How to make pizza:

1. Gather ingredients (pizza dough, pizza sauce, cheese, toppings)

2. Preheat the oven 475°F

3. Prepare a baking sheet

4. Prepare the dough

- Flour the surface

- Roll/Stretch the dough

- Place the dough on a baking sheet

5. Add sauce & cheese

- Spread a layer of pizza sauce evenly over the dough

- Sprinkle a layer of cheese on top of the sauce

6. Add toppings

- Evenly distribute the chosen toppings over the cheese

7. Bake the pizza

- Place the pizza in the preheated oven

- Bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the pizza looks ready

8. Finish up to serve

- Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool

- Cut the pizza into slices

- Serve the pizza
